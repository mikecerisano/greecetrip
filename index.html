Athens & Santorini Trip Itinerary

📅 May 11-17

🏛 Athens (May 11-14)

📍 May 11 – Arrival & Exploring Athens

🏠 Airbnb in Athens

Plaka & Anafiotika – 🏛 Google Maps - Wander through this charming, historic neighborhood filled with whitewashed houses, colorful doors, and a relaxed island-like atmosphere.

Areopagus Hill – 🌄 Google Maps - A rocky outcrop near the Acropolis offering breathtaking sunset views over Athens.

SENSE Restaurant – 🍽 Google Maps - A rooftop dining experience with panoramic views of the illuminated Acropolis.

Brettos Bar – 🍷 Google Maps - The oldest distillery in Athens, known for its vast selection of Greek spirits and cozy ambiance.

🏠 May 12 – Ancient Athens & Hidden Gems

Acropolis & Parthenon – 🏛 Google Maps - The iconic temple dedicated to Athena, a must-see architectural wonder with centuries of history.

Temple of Olympian Zeus & Hadrian’s Arch – 🏛 Google Maps - One of the largest temples of the ancient world, showcasing impressive Corinthian columns.

Monastiraki Flea Market – 🛍 Google Maps - A vibrant marketplace with unique souvenirs, antiques, and local food stalls.

A for Athens Rooftop – 🍸 Google Maps - A popular rooftop bar offering breathtaking views of the Acropolis at sunset.

🏝 Santorini (May 14-17)

📍 May 14 – Arrival & Exploring Fira

🏠 Airbnb in Santorini

Fira – 🏙 Google Maps - The capital of Santorini, boasting stunning cliffside views, boutique shops, and vibrant nightlife.

Pelican Kipos – 🍷 Google Maps - A hidden gem in Fira, this wine bar offers a cozy atmosphere and a selection of Santorini wines.

Kastro Oia Restaurant – 🌅 Google Maps - One of the best places to watch the famous Santorini sunset while enjoying a delicious meal.

🛥 May 16 – Sunset Catamaran Cruise

Perivolos Beach – 🏖 Google Maps - A long stretch of black sand beach with crystal-clear waters, lined with beach bars and restaurants offering sunbeds and umbrellas. Perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and enjoying a relaxed beach day.

Sunset Catamaran Cruise – ⛵ Viator Link - Departs from Vlychada Port at 3:30 PM. Includes stops at Red Beach, White Beach, and the hot springs. Enjoy a BBQ dinner, unlimited drinks, and a stunning sunset view from the water. Returns to Fira in the evening.

🍇 May 17 – Wine Tasting & Departure

Santo Wines Winery – 🍇 Google Maps - A breathtaking cliffside winery offering panoramic views of the caldera and top-tier local wines.

✈ Flight from Santorini to Athens, then to Sweden for Mike and back to Texas for Daiane (unless you want to come to Sweden lol).
